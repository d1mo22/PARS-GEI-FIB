## FIB Specializations

The GEI degree at FIB offers 5 specializations:
1. **Computacio** - Theoretical CS, algorithms, AI, graphics
2. **Enginyeria del Software** - Software design and development
3. **Sistemes d'Informacio** - Information systems and databases
4. **Enginyeria de Computadors** - Hardware and systems
5. **Tecnologies de la Informacio** - Networks and distributed systems
