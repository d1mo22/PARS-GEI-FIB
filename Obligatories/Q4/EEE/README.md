# EEE - Empresa i Entorn Economic (Business and Economic Environment)

## About the Subject

This 6-credit mandatory course combines economics and business fundamentals for Computer Engineering students. The course is divided into two main parts.

### Main Topics

**Part 1 - Economics:**
- Economic concepts (inflation, GDP, employment)
- Money, taxation, and public finance
- Keynesian vs. classical economic thought
- Fiscal and monetary policy

**Part 2 - Business:**
- Company types, management, and organization
- Functional areas: HR, finance, marketing, operations
- Business ethics and regulatory compliance
- Corporate governance and social responsibility

### Learning Objectives
- Develop critical reasoning about economic policy decisions
- Understand business as a system with interconnected departments
- Knowledge of financial concepts for project viability analysis
- Awareness of IT applications in organizations
